Use Lipa na Mpesa
  • 1: Go to the M-Pesa menu on your mobile phone.
  • 2: Choose the "Lipa na M-Pesa" option from the menu.
  • 3: Select "Paybill" from the sub-menu.
  • 4: Input the I&M Bank Paybill number 542542 in the Paybill field.
  • 5: Enter 02405733356150 I&M Bank account number in the "account number" field.
  • 6: Input the exact amount you wish to deposit or if auto-selected, there is no need
  • 7: Input your M-Pesa PIN to authorize the transaction.
  • 8: Copy the M-Pesa confirmation code once the transaction is successful.
  • 9: Paste the M-Pesa confirmation code below.
  • 10: Click "Confirm" to complete the process and your account will be updated.
  • Note: Kindly contact the admin incase of payment and verification failures
